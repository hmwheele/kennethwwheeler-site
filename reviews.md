**[Review of Rev. Kenneth Wheeler's *US: The Resurrection of American Terror*](https://www.patheos.com/blogs/ecopreacher/2023/08/review-rev-kenneth-wheeler-us-resurrection-of-american-terror/)**

> A profound and prophetic memoir that every White Christian needs to read. Wheeler, a retired ordained minister in the ELCA who served for 40 years, draws on his experience of the whiteness of the Lutheran Church in America and how it has perpetuated the sin of racism.

## ![Living Lutheran](https://www.livinglutheran.org/favicon.ico) Living Lutheran

**[A Prophetic Call to Action](https://www.livinglutheran.org/a-prophetic-call-to-action/)**

*Living Lutheran Interview*

> Wheeler was compelled to write the book after connecting the trauma he'd experienced under Jim Crow segregation to the rise in Christian nationalism he'd seen in recent years. A retired ELCA pastor who served the Greater Milwaukee Synod for 18 years as an assistant to the bishop, Wheeler drew on his experience as a Black pastor in predominantly white church spaces. Grounded in Scripture, *US* challenges readers to reclaim the fullness of their humanity.

## ![LA Sentinel](https://lasentinel.net/favicon.ico) Los Angeles Sentinel

**[Reverend Dr. Kenneth W. Wheeler to Talk About Greatest Threat to the Church](https://lasentinel.net/reverend-dr-kenneth-w-wheeler-to-talk-about-greatest-threat-to-the-church.html)**

*Los Angeles Sentinel*

> Wheeler argues that white supremacy is the greatest existential threat that faces America and the American church. During the COVID pandemic, Wheeler brought to life a book he had been carrying for a long time — "a book that probably, I had been carrying around in my system, in my body, for probably 70 years." Wheeler presented his work at Grace United Methodist Church in Los Angeles, leading a community workshop on what he identifies as the biggest threat to the church.
